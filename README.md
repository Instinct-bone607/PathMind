# 🧭 PathMind - Understand exactly how users navigate software

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_PathMind-Blue?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/Instinct-bone607/PathMind/releases)

PathMind records your screen actions and turns them into organized knowledge. It tracks each click and interaction to show you how your product workflow functions. The application uses artificial intelligence to analyze these paths. It then produces a structured database that you can use with your own document systems.

## 🛠 Features

PathMind simplifies the way you document product behavior.

- Screen Recording: Start the app to capture every step you take in any desktop program.
- Visual Analysis: The tool identifies the buttons, menus, and screens you interact with during your session.
- Knowledge Export: It organizes the data into a format that works with AI-powered search systems.
- Workflow Mapping: It creates a visual history of how a user moves from start to finish.
- Data Privacy: The processing happens on your local machine to keep your sensitive product data secure.

## 🚀 How to Use PathMind

Once you install the program, you can start tracking your workflows.

1. Open the PathMind icon from your desktop or start menu.
2. Select your target application from the list inside the dashboard.
3. Press the Start button to begin recording your actions.
4. Perform your usual tasks, such as clicking buttons or moving through menus, as if you were a new user.
5. Press the Stop button when you reach the end of your workflow.
6. Wait for the status indicator to show that the analysis is complete.
7. Save your result to a folder of your choice as a structured knowledge file.

## 🧠 Understanding the Analysis

PathMind translates your clicks into clear, text-based guides. When you record a path, the software evaluates the visual elements on your screen. It recognizes the labels on your buttons and the titles of your windows. It then builds a logical map of your software.

This resulting file represents your workflow in a way that AI search tools understand. You can feed this data into your knowledge base to help team members find answers about how specific features work.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you experience issues, these steps often resolve them.

- If the application starts slowly, close other background programs that use high amounts of memory.
- If the recorder misses steps, ensure the target application is visible on your screen and not minimized.
- If the AI analysis reports an error, check that your internet connection is active, as the software needs this to process the visual data.
- If you cannot find the saved file, check the default PathMind folder in your Documents directory.

PathMind respects the importance of your internal workflows. The application captures screen data to interpret your product paths. ## 💡 Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use PathMind for web applications?
Yes, PathMind tracks any activity on your desktop, including web browsers.

Does it support multiple monitors?
PathMind captures the primary display where the target application is active.

What is a RAG-ready knowledge base?
RAG stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation. It is a way for AI to look at your specific files to provide accurate answers. PathMind creates these files so your AI tools can learn about your product.

Are there limits to the recording length?
Long recordings take more time to analyze. Start and stop the recorder for individual tasks to get the best results.

Can I export recordings to other formats?
The software produces standardized text files optimized for knowledge management systems. You can open these with almost any document editor.
